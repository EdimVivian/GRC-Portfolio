# Audit Checklist
An audit checklist is a list of items an auditor reviews to confirm that policies, standards, and controls are being followed.

It helps verify compliance and highlight weaknesses.

Example:

| Control Area | Requirement | Evidence | Status |
|--------------|-------------|----------|--------|
| Policies | Policies documented and approved | Policy repository | ✔ |
| Access Control | Quarterly access reviews | Review logs | ✔ |
| Incident Response | IR plan tested annually | Test report | ✖ |
| Backups | Daily backups performed | Backup logs | ✔ |
