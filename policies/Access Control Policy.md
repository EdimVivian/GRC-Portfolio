# Access Control Policy
Explains how users get access to systems and ensures people only access what they need.

## Purpose
To ensure users have appropriate access based on job responsibilities.

## Policy Statement
- Access must follow least privilege.
- All accounts must be uniquely identifiable.
- Access reviews must occur quarterly.
- Privileged access requires multi-factor authentication (MFA).

## Responsibilities
- IT: Provision/deprovision accounts.
- Managers: Approve access.
- Security: Monitor and review access.

## Enforcement
Violations may lead to access removal or disciplinary action.
