
# Vendor Management Policy
Ensures third‑party vendors meet security requirements before working with the organisation.

## Purpose
Ensure third-party vendors meet security, privacy, and compliance requirements.

## Scope
All vendors providing services, software, or handling organisational data.

## Policy
- Vendors must undergo security due diligence.
- Contracts must include security, confidentiality, and compliance clauses.
- High-risk vendors require annual reviews.
- Access provided to vendors must follow least privilege.

## Responsibilities
- Procurement: Vendor onboarding.
- Security: Risk assessment.
- Legal: Contract review.

## Enforcement
Non-compliance may result in termination of vendor engagement
