# Data Privacy Policy

## Purpose
Protect personal data and ensure compliance with GDPR and privacy regulations.

## Policy
- Personal data must be collected lawfully and minimally.
- Data subjects have rights to access, correction, and deletion.
- Personal data must be encrypted at rest and in transit.
- Breaches must be reported within regulatory timelines.

## Responsibilities
- DPO: Oversight.
- Security: Technical controls.
- Employees: Proper handling of personal data.
