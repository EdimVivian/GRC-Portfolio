# Information Security Policy

## Purpose
To establish the organisation’s approach to protecting information assets.

## Scope
All employees, contractors, systems, and data.

## Policy Statement
- Information must be protected from unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.
- Security controls must align with ISO 27001.
- Users must follow all security procedures and report incidents immediately.

## Responsibilities
- Management: Ensure resources and enforcement.
- IT/Security Team: Implement controls.
- Employees: Follow policies and report issues.

## Enforcement
Non-compliance may result in disciplinary action.
