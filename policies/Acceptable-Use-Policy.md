# Acceptable Use Policy
Tells employees what they can and cannot do with company devices, networks, and data.

## Purpose
Define acceptable use of company systems.

## Acceptable Use
- Use systems for authorised business activities.
- Protect confidential information.
- Follow password and device security rules.

## Prohibited Use
- Installing unauthorised software.
- Accessing illegal or harmful content.
- Sharing credentials.

## Enforcement
Breaches may result in disciplinary action.
